About Us

We are a family-owned chicken farm dedicated to providing the best quality chickens. Our farm is located in the countryside, where our chickens are raised in a healthy and natural environment.

Our Services

  • Chicken Sales: We offer a variety of chicken breeds for sale, all raised in a healthy environment.
  • Farm Tours: Experience our farm firsthand with guided tours.
  • Educational Workshops: Learn about chicken farming and care through our workshops.
  • Delivery Services: We provide delivery services for bulk orders.

Our Products

  • Fresh Eggs: Organic and free-range eggs available in various quantities.
  • Chicken Meat: High-quality, fresh chicken meat processed on-site.
  • Chicken Feed: Nutritious feed for your chickens, available for purchase.
  • Farm Equipment: A range of equipment and supplies for chicken farming.

Our Brands

  • Golden Egg: Our premium brand for organic eggs.
  • Healthy Hen: A trusted name in chicken feed and supplements.
  • Farm Fresh: Our brand for high-quality chicken meat.
  • EcoFarm: Sustainable and eco-friendly farming equipment.
